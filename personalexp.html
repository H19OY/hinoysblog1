Why Sundarban is the magical wonders?

The Sundarbans world heritage sites – home to magnificent animal on land is Royal Bengal Tiger, spotted deer, wild boars, macaque, barking deer, jungle and fishing cat, otters, water monitor and snakes. there are 290 species of birds and mostly seen are – pond heron, purple heron, egrets, cattle egrets, greater egret, open billed stork, smaller adjutant stork, brahmini kite, fish eagle, magpie robin, jungle moyna, bulbul, wood peckers, bea eater, leaf bird, 9 species of kingfisher, drongo, racket tallied drongo, rose ringed parakeet, dove, parrot, mangrove pitta, and crow. The word Sundarban means ‘beautiful forest’. It is a immeasurable area in the Ganges delta comprising a network of 108 swampy, two-third low-lying landmass amidst one- third water bodies of 450 river, canal and creeks, mainly fresh water flows north to south direction and sediments deposited in the delta covered with enormous brackish mud flats contain 28 species true mangrove plants boast of a variety of flora and diversified faunal array to bring an unequaled diverse wildlife Sundarban Mangrove ecosystems are of massive ecological significance which Situated mostly in southwest Bangladesh, a small portion of it lies in India. The Indian part of the forest is estimated to be about 19 percent, while the Bangladeshi part is 81 percent. Three wildlife Sanctuaries superb by sedges, legumes, algal, grasses a variety of terrestrial shrubs and Magical mangrove trees where stealthily spotted deer, Macaque and wild boar survive and ferocious Bengal tiger its roam beyond various wildlife. Its declared as a UNESCO world natural heritage site in 1997. Every year many travelers expedition in and around of these wildlife sanctuaries and mostly viewed sites – namely – KATKA, KACHIKHALI, Dubla island and HERON POINT.

Best Day of My Life


A birthday party is a happy and attractive occasion in a modern society. It is observed on one’s birthday. It comes once every year with joy and merriment. I celebrated my last birthday on 1st December 2017. I was 17. It was the fifteenth birthday of mine. I invited my friends and relatives. It was held at our residence at Gulshan. On this occasion, our drawing room was nicely decorated with flowers and festoons. The house wore a festive look. We ordered a birthday cake. My father bought a fine new dress for me and my mother was going to give me a pleasant surprise. The cake arrived in time. Different items of foods were placed on the table. My friends and guests began to arrive by twos and threes. By 5 o’clock everybody arrived. We all stood around the dining table. I merrily cut the cake. A round of applause followed. Plates full of delicious food items were handed over to the guests. We ate them in the midst of merriments, songs, and music. The camera of my brother was clicking on. He took pictures of rare moments. The presents brought by my friends and others were given to me. I thanked them all. I enjoyed the day very much. The party was rounded off with a chorus ‘Happy birthday to you’.
